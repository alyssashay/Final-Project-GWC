THE ISSUE

Animals all over the world lack warm homes to live in, ecpecially dogs and cats. People usually adopt puppies/kittens and disregard the older dogs/cats. But if any animal doesn't get adopted, they end up spending the rest of their lives in a cage and eventually be put down.

Approximately 6.5 million companion animals enter U.S. animal shelters nationwide every year. Of those, approximately 3.3 million are dogs and 3.2 million are cats.Approximately 3.2 million shelter animals are adopted each year (1.6 million dogs and 1.6 million cats).
